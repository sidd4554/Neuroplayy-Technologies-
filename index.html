Privacy Policy

Introduction

Torque Legends is designed for a general audience, including children under the age of 13 and under 9. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with applicable laws such as COPPA.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location from users. We may collect limited non-personal information such as device type, performance data, and crash logs strictly for improving the game.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 or under 9. If we discover that any personal information has been collected unintentionally, we will delete it immediately. Parents can contact us for any concerns regarding their child's data.

Use of Data

Non-personal data is used only to improve gameplay performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience. No data is used for behavioral advertising or profiling of children.

Third-Party Services

Torque Legends may use limited third-party services such as Google Play Services for basic functionality. These services are configured to comply with child privacy protection standards and do not collect personal data from children.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect all data. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when required. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: support@torquelegends.com
